Scott Rayson "Race" Briggs is an up and coming web developer in the Austin, Texas area. Born in Houston, Texas in 1994, he moved to and lived in the DFW area for most of his life. After high school, he moved to Austin to attend the University of Texas and never left. He has had a fascination with computers since he was young, held previous jobs in IT support, and is now ready to take on the coding world! He is a diligent worker with a great attitude towards learning and collaboration.

He is currently studying web development with Eric Scott at the UT Coding Bootcamp. In a matter of weeks, he will be proficient in full stack development, altough he prefers working on the front end. He will know the building blocks of the web, including HTML, CSS, Javascript, Node.js, React, API management, and database management. He is a quick and determined student, and has no qualms learning anything a job could throw at him.

In his free time, Race enjoys listening to music, playing video games, and trying out new and exotic foods. His favorite video game is Dota 2, which he has accumulated over 6000 hours playing. Whether you're looking for a new hire or a new Steam friend, Race would be the perfect choice for you!
